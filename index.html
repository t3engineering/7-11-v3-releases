Release 0.05 (1)

This is our first build to QA. Our versioning convention is 0.NN (M) where NN is the sprint number and M is the build number in this sprint. So the 7th build of the 12th sprint would be 0.12 (7).

We follow this convention until we get to 1.x, where we then follow regular versioning.
